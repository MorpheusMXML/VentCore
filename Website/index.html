Supports You and Your Patient.

VentCore introduces a new generation of mobile live-saving equipment. Our 3-In-1 Device is specially designed to support paramedics and doctors in all situations. By combining 3 devices into one, we want to reduce the weight you carry and the sensory overload you endure by minimizing redundant settings and alarms.

Watch Trailer



Combines the advantages of three devices into one.

VentCore displays all necessary Parameter Values and Graphs for you on one display. You are able to switch between modes and adjust settings like you are used to. With a smart audible behaviour, VentCore seeks to minimize the possible stress you and your patient experience during an emergency.

Features designed for You:

Modern UI/UX Design

VentCore displays attributes and functionalities of 3 devices on one screen with a new, modern and enjoyable interface. Benefit from the advantages of an intuitive touch display in combination with hardware buttons.

Switch Modes

Switch easily between the different modes: Monitoring, Ventilation and Defibrillation. VentCore displays mode-dependent attributes to you without losing overview of most important parameters and graphs.

Alarmmanagement

VentCore aims to reduce the sensory overload caused by three individual devices. Explore the new mute behaviour of repetitive alarms and concentrate on reanimation, while the systems mutes auditory alarms.

Customization

VentCore allows you to customize the screen according to your preferences. Add parameters and graphs whenever you need them or remove them easily. You can also switch between a Dark- and Light-Mode.

Settings and Presets

Set parameter limits directly for one or for all parameters at once. Adjust Ventilation and Defibrillation settings whenever you need to. Choose patient presets or enter individual values for documentation.

Patient Scenarios

Test the device using multiple scenario with different alarms and interact with the device like you are in a real emergency. Each stage contains simulated data with various levels of severity.
